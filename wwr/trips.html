The shakedown

The shakedown is our most adrenaline-pumping trip, venture through strong currents and go down the river at full speed, facing medium waterfalls and raising your adrenaline to the maximum, after this trip there is no way not to come away shaken.

Force of nature

If you want a moderate adventure but still with some level of adrenaline, this is the perfect tour for you. Take a trip down this river where the currents are fast and incredibly strong. Here you will feel the true force of nature.

Slow down the river

If you don't like adrenaline and prefer a fun ride where you'll get a little wet and enjoy nature, slow the river is perfect for you, here you go down the river in a calmer current but without missing out on adrenaline and fun.
